# KoiMoe Copy Guide

This document is the reference for public-facing text, naming, atmosphere words, and image-search taste for this personal blog. Use it when writing page titles, subtitles, notices, empty states, button copy, admin login text, image prompts, or short descriptions.

## Core Name

- Primary site title: `KoiMoe Diary`
- Preferred Japanese subtitle: `恋と萌えの小さな場所`
- Optional Japanese-style title: `こいもえ日和`
- Optional Chinese title: `恋萌手帖`
- Domain identity: `koimoe.com`
- Author name: `莉莉姆`

Use `KoiMoe Diary` as the safest default title. It keeps the `koi` / `moe` meaning while feeling lighter and less direct than using `恋萌` everywhere.

When copy, UI labels, metadata, profile cards, article bylines, examples, or seeded content need an author name, use `莉莉姆`.

## Core Feeling

The blog should feel like a small personal place for:

- heartbeats, crushes, and romantic softness
- cute things and moe culture
- anime-adjacent personal taste
- diary fragments, memories, links, images, and small tools
- a warm return point rather than a public product

The sentence `恋と萌えの小さな場所` is the emotional center: soft, personal, a little romantic, and gently two-dimensional without being loud.

## Tone

Prefer text that is:

- light, intimate, and calm
- slightly poetic, but still natural
- cute without being childish
- Japanese-inspired without forcing every line into Japanese
- personal rather than promotional

Avoid text that sounds:

- corporate, SaaS-like, or framework-demo-like
- too technical on public pages
- too explicit about "anime", "otaku", or "moe" unless the context naturally calls for it
- overly dramatic, overly sugary, or roleplay-heavy

## Naming Notes

`KoiMoe` works well because:

- `koi` can suggest love, heartbeats, or the Japanese word `恋`
- `moe` suggests cute/anime affection and the feeling of being charmed
- together they are direct enough to be memorable, but softer in roman letters than `恋萌`

`恋萌` is acceptable as a Chinese shorthand, but it can feel a little direct. Prefer using it in secondary contexts, such as:

- `恋萌手帖`
- `恋萌小站`
- a Chinese explanation of what KoiMoe means

## Page Copy Guidance

For public pages:

- Keep headings short and atmospheric.
- Let the UI feel like a personal diary, not a content platform.
- Use `Diary`, `Notes`, `Archive`, `Fragments`, `Garden`, `Room`, or `日和` style words when appropriate.
- Prefer "small place", "soft diary", "tiny heartbeats", "fragments", "favorite things", and "gentle days" style wording.

For admin and login pages:

- The admin area can be practical, but it should still feel connected to the public site.
- Login copy may be more intimate and atmospheric than other admin screens.
- Good login line: `Welcome back, dream keeper.`
- Other login line options:
  - `Welcome back to your little place.`
  - `The diary is waiting softly.`
  - `Back to the room of tiny heartbeats.`

For empty states:

- Avoid blunt technical empties like `No data`.
- Use calm, compact text:
  - `No fragments yet.`
  - `Nothing has bloomed here yet.`
  - `This page is still waiting for its first note.`

## Image And Material Taste

When looking for temporary or long-term images, prefer:

- soft cherry blossoms
- pale sky, spring light, and white/pink architecture
- Japanese streets, station platforms, school roads, cafes, bedrooms, windows, and afternoon light
- romantic visual-novel atmosphere
- calm anime background art without a dominant character face in the center
- horizontal wallpaper compositions that leave room for text or a centered glass panel

Avoid:

- high-saturation character posters
- busy collages
- heavy black/red/purple cyber styling
- images where a face, logo, or body is directly behind the login panel
- images that feel too erotic, aggressive, or noisy for a personal diary login page

For yande.re searches, useful starting queries:

- `rating:safe wallpaper sakura order:score`
- `rating:safe wallpaper scenery order:score`
- `rating:safe landscape sakura order:favcount`
- `rating:safe anime room window order:score`
- `rating:safe visual_novel wallpaper sakura order:score`

For the login background specifically, prioritize:

- `rating:safe`
- `wallpaper`
- width at least `1920`
- horizontal composition
- light color values
- no strong subject in the center
